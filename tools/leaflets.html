Patient Leaflet Finder

Type a condition — get the best UK patient leaflet plus 2-3 alternates from specialist charities. Copy the link to paste into AccuRx, an SMS, or an email.

Browse by specialty
Reference tool, not a directory of validated leaflets. Links anchor to canonical landing pages at established UK patient-information organisations (NHS, Versus Arthritis, BHF, BAD, Diabetes UK, Mind, RCOG, Macmillan, etc). Always preview the leaflet yourself before sharing it with a patient — content changes, and the right leaflet for the patient depends on language, literacy, and context. Translation support varies by organisation; NHS pages support browser-level translation widgets in 100+ languages.
