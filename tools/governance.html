How the update process works

  1. Alerts. Subscribe to NICE "Save & alert" emails, the MHRA Drug Safety Update, and the UKHSA/Green Book bulletins so changes reach you the day they publish.
  2. Watch the sources. Each guideline above links to its official page — check its last-revised date against the one recorded here.
  3. Review & update. When a source has changed, update the affected cases, pathways and prescriptions, then press Mark reviewed here.
  4. Persist. Press Export updated dates and paste the result into assets/review-register.json so the new dates are permanent for everyone, not just this device.
  5. Rolling 12-month sweep. Even with no alert, anything untouched for 12 months turns amber then red — a safety-net so nothing silently ages.
