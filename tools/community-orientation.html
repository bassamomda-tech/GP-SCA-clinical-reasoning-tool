Community orientation — who's who in the community team

General practice works through a wide multidisciplinary team. Knowing each role — what they do and when to involve them — is a core RCGP capability and a frequent SCA theme. Each card describes the role and when to refer or work with them.

Educational use only. Team structures, titles and referral routes vary widely by practice, PCN, ICB and UK nation. Check your local directory of services, PCN roles (ARRS) and community provider arrangements for exact pathways.
