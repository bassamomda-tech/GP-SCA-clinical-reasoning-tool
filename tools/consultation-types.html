Consultation types — the ways a GP sees patients

The settings and structured consultations that make up general practice — from chronic-disease and cancer-care reviews to advance care planning, the Gold Standards Framework, end-of-life care, home visits, telephone and out-of-hours work, extended access and urgent treatment centres. Each card sets out what it is and the practical rules that govern it.

Educational use only. Service models, contractual requirements (GMS/PCN DES) and QOF/IIF details change and vary across the UK nations and ICBs. Check current NHS England contract guidance, your practice protocols and local commissioning arrangements before acting.
