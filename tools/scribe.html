Consult Scribe

Record the consultation as it happens — pause when you're interrupted — then ✦ Enhance the transcript (AI fixes speech-to-text mishears, punctuation and Doctor/Patient labels) and turn it into a ready-to-paste GP record in your chosen note style and care setting (GP surgery · OOH · UTC), formatted for EMIS Web, SystmOne or a generic SOAP note. Generates examination, impression, plan, safety-netting, a referral draft, a patient summary, prescription ideas and admin tasks — flags NICE NG12 two-week-wait criteria, highlights missed red flags, and the SCA prep tab marks the consultation like an RCGP examiner. Add custom instructions to make every note match your style.

⚙️ Best on the live site, in Chrome. Live dictation needs the microphone (Chrome/Edge) and note generation needs the assistant connection. Never dictate or paste real patient-identifiable data into a tool. Always read, edit and verify the generated record before it enters the clinical system — you remain responsible for its accuracy. Educational / drafting aid only.
