Educational use only — the DVLA guide is the source of truth. Rules summarise the DVLA's Assessing fitness to drive: a guide for medical professionals. It is the patient's legal duty to notify the DVLA (DVA in Northern Ireland) where required; your duty is to advise them clearly, document that advice, and follow GMC guidance if a patient continues to drive against advice. Group 2 (LGV/PCV) standards are stricter throughout. Thresholds change — verify the exact current criteria and time periods in the DVLA guide on gov.uk before advising.
