The consultation spine — complaint + diagnosis, the whole consultation

Enter the presenting complaint and your provisional diagnosis (e.g. headache → migraine, chest pain → angina) and tap Generate full consultation — every section fills with an editable, NICE-aligned draft: history, red flags, examination, impression, investigations, management & prescription, referral, safety-net and follow-up. You just edit the [bracketed] parts for your patient. It also pulls the matching casebook case, protocol, algorithm and tools, and assembles a structured note, a patient-friendly summary and indicative codes to paste into EMIS/SystmOne or AccuRx.
