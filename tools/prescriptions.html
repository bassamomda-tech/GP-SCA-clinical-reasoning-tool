Ready prescriptions — dose, duration, counselling, EMIS copy-paste

Filterable grid of fully-formed prescription bundles. Each opens to the full plan — indication · contraindications · drug table (dose · freq · route · duration) · alternative regimens · counselling points · follow-up · red flags · copy-paste text for EMIS / SystmOne · NICE CKS source links.

Educational use only. Every prescription mirrors NICE CKS / NICE NG / FSRH / BNF guidance as cited per card. Always verify dose, contraindications, drug interactions, and your local formulary before prescribing — and confirm patient-specific factors (renal function, pregnancy, allergies, concomitant medications). Not a substitute for clinical judgement.
