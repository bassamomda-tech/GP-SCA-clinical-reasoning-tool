How it countsTurning reading into appraisal-ready CPD

Step 1 · Automatic

Read — it logs itself

Open any case, pathway, protocol or tool. Active reading time is counted and converted to credits (≈ 1 credit per hour). Listening on the Audios player counts too.

Auto timed
Step 2 · Reflect

Add a structured reflection

The GMC values reflection, not attendance. Each entry prompts you for what you learned, what you'll change, and any further learning need — the appraisal-standard format.

3-part prompt
Step 3 · Broaden

Log external learning too

Add courses, webinars, podcasts, journal reading and SEA meetings so your whole CPD year lives in one place — and vary topics across the GP curriculum.

+ Add CPD
Step 4 · Evidence

Print your record

A clean, dated CPD record per appraisal year — your name, declared credits, every activity and its reflection. Ready to upload to FourteenFish, PReP or your portfolio.
